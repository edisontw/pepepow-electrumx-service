Important Safety Caveats:

This is not an invoice database. It does not create, reserve, store, or reconcile merchant invoices. It only checks the current balance and transaction history of a single address. For reliable use, generate a unique, fresh receiving address for each payment and avoid spending from that address before the payment is confirmed. Reusing addresses or spending from the address can make balance-based payment checks ambiguous.
